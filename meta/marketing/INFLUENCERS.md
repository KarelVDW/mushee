# Sheemu — Influencer & Creator Outreach Lists

Companion to [ICP.md](./ICP.md). Three niches, one per ICP, targeting the
**~10k–100k subscriber band** (affordable micro/mid-tier). Every creator below was
verified to exist on 2026-07-11, with subscriber counts read from their public
channel pages on that date — YouTube rounds to three significant figures and
counts drift, so **re-verify at outreach time**. Rates in this band vary wildly;
always ask for a media kit before pricing a deal.

Five creators were independently surfaced by two research passes (marked ↔) —
that's a signal of strong fit, and each is listed once under its best-fit niche.

## How to use these lists

1. **Wave 1 (beta → launch):** pick 3–5 per niche with the clearest demo-format fit
   (suggested: Aural Skills Guru, Sharon Bill, Jeremy Siskind / Worship Ministry
   Training, SongTown, Emma McGann / Tim Topham, Carly Walton, Nicola Cantan) and
   offer free Studio accounts + affiliate codes before paid deals — in this band
   many creators will demo a genuinely novel tool for product access alone.
2. **Track per-creator attribution** with distinct promo codes/UTM links so wave 2
   spends against measured conversion, not subscriber counts.
3. The over-band "later wave" names in each niche's notes are the natural next
   step once CAC per niche is known.
